Repercussions on the city

After looking at the more dangerous areas in Chicago in terms of crime rate, we will try to see if there is a relation between the high crime rate of an area and other factors that correspond to the quality of life such as the quality of restaurants, and the number of flu clinics. Moreover we'll try to determine if those zones are in general very frequented or not by using our Taxi Rides history. To perform our analysis, we decided to consider Chicago at the level of its Communitiy Areas.

Flu Clinics

Below is the geographical distribution of the Flu Clinics accross the city :



By looking at the geographical distribution of the clinics in Chicago, we can see that there is bigger amount of clinics that gave flu vaccines in the Northern West part of the city. There is a clear difference between North and South by means of the number of clinics. We can then suppose that the Northern part is wealthier than the Southern part. areas 32, 8, and 6 are the ones with the most number of clinics. We can assume that those areas which are close to the lake are wealthier than the ones in the East Side or South. Our assumption is valid as the most frequent crime in those areas is Theft.
