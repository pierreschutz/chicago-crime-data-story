Conclusion

Through this analysis, we learned a lot about Crime in Chicago, its distribution over the city. We were able to find the areas where organized crime types are reported more frequently. We also observed correlations between life quality factors and criminality is multiple areas. With the help of the datasets provided by the city of Chicago, we built meaningful models that could help to solve real-world problems.
